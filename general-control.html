General Pest Control

Godrej ICare Pest Control provides a wide spectrum of pest control services for General Pests such as cockroaches, bedbugs, ants, ticks, rodents, flies, bees, wasps, scorpions, snakes, etc.

We are committed to protecting health and the environment, so we always use low-toxic, stain-free, eco-friendly, and pet-friendly pesticides. All pesticides are approved by the Ministry of Environment and Climate Change (MOCCAE) and will be used as directed by the manufacturer and the Dubai Municipality - Public Health and Pest Control Section.

All our technicians are trained in integrated pest management (IPM), and we consistently adopt new technologies to enhance our services. Malabar Pest Control is committed to providing the best possible service to create a community of satisfied customers.

By aligning with the spirit of the UN Climate Change Conference, Malabar Pest Control actively promote the use of organic chemicals and non-chemical methods in order to minimize the reliance on chemical-based solutions. Our aim is to reduce the overall usage of chemicals while effectively addressing pest control needs.
